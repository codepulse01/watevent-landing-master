About Us - Watevent

Struggling to find local events? Watevent's FREE event website & app aggregates events across cities. Discover music, festivals & more - all in one place!


Our Story

Welcome to Watevent! I'm Ari Wasch, the founder of Watevent. After moving between various cities, I found it challenging to keep up with interesting events due to the multitude of websites and platforms available. This inspired me to create Watevent, a platform designed to streamline the event discovery process by bringing all local events into one convenient event listing app.


Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the process of discovering local events, creating a seamless and engaging experience for our users. We aim to foster connections within communities by making it easier for people to find and participate in events that interest them through our event website and event listing app.

Our Vision

We envision a world where discovering and participating in local events is effortless and enjoyable. As we expand, our goal is to reach more cities, helping more people connect with their communities and enrich their lives through engaging activities. This vision will be achieved through our user-friendly event website and our comprehensive event listing app.

What We Provide

Watevent is dedicated to bringing you the largest selection of local events in one convenient event listing app. Our services include:

Event Aggregation:

We gather event information from a wide range of city-related websites and social media platforms to provide you with the most comprehensive event & listings.

Daily Updates:

Our database is updated daily to ensure you have access to the latest events on our event website and event listing app.

Advanced Search and Filters:

Easily find events that match your interests with our intuitive search and filter options on our user-friendly event website and event listing app.

User-Friendly Interface:

Enjoy a smooth and responsive browsing experience, designed to make discovering events effortless on our event website and event listing app.

How Our Services Work

Data Collection:

Watevent continuously scans and compiles event information from various city-related websites and social media platforms.

Event Curation:

Our team curates and organizes the collected data, ensuring it is accurate and up-to-date for our event website and event listing app.

User Access:

Through our app, users can browse, search, and filter events based on their interests and preferences.

Stay Informed:

Users receive real-time updates on events, ensuring they never miss out on exciting activities in their city through our event website and event listing app.
